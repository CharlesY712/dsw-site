Mentor Sessions

Looking for some free advice? Our mentors are here to help.

Colorado’s top entrepreneurs are volunteering their time and talent to meet with Denver Startup Week attendees. Sign up for a 20-minute, 1:1 meeting with mentors from all walks of the startup world. All mentor office hours will be held at Basecamp, 1201 16th Street, @ Larimer in Downtown Denver

Mentor Office Hours are organized by the Entrepreneurs Foundation of Colorado -- a network of Colorado entrepreneurs whose companies share a common commitment to pledge a portion of founding equity or a portion of annual profits to the communities in which they reside. www.efcolorado.org

Schedule: Monday-Friday, September 16-20
Monday-Friday: 10:00am-12:00pm & 2:00pm-4:00pm

Leadership Spotlights

The Leadership Spotlight Sessions at Basecamp provide an intimate look at high-profile leaders in the startup community. Taking place on the Basecamp Leadership stage, these sessions will feature interviews and Q&A with startup founders and community leaders. Each themed session aims to bring out a unique story and spotlight the history, success and lessons learned from each leader. Join us from 12:00pm-2:00pm, Tuesday-Friday, to hear from those who are driving innovation in Denver today. Leadership Spotlights will feature 3-4 speakers each day. Daily themes include: Faces of Entrepreneurship, Emerging Stars, Healthy Entrepreneurial Eco-Systems and Leaders of the Startup Scene.

Schedule: Tuesday-Friday, September 17-20 12:00pm-2:00pm
